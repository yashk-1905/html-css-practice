Comprehensive Foundation

The K11 Personal Trainer’s Course is the foundational course in Exercise Science. It provides comprehensive theoretical and practical education, equipping students with the knowledge-base and tools to professionally practice as Qualified, Certified Personal Trainers in the Fitness Industry.

Globally Recognised Qualification

The K11 Diploma in Personal Training (DPT) course is internationally recognised by the Register for Exercise Professionals, India (REPS India). This grants students Registered Exercise Professional status. A Personal Trainer with REPS India Membership belongs to a global fraternity of Personal Trainers educated as per Global Standards laid down by the International Confederation of Registers for Exercise Professionals (iCREPS).

Personalised Attention

To provide personalised attention and adequate practice time during the Practical Training module, irrespective of the theory batch size, we maintain a 1:6 Teacher-to-Student Ratio. To achieve this, we divide the students into groups of 6, with each group assigned one K11 Master Trainer.

Global Career Opportunities

K11 has a dedicated Sudent Placement Assistance Cell (SPAC) that assists students in kick-starting their careers in the Fitness Industry, both internationally as well as nationally.

  • As a K11 DPT Certificate holder and a member of REPS India under Category-A (Personal Trainer), students are eligible to apply for Fitness Industry jobs in countries such as UAE, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Poland, and Ireland, thus offering a truly global career perspective.

  • A K11 CPT Certificate holder can only apply for Fitness Industry jobs within India.

At any given point in time, there are over 500 active Job vacancies available for K11 students across India and over 60 vacancies internationally.

Equal Emphasis on Theory and Practical Training

K11 has been instrumental in formulating the Personal Training Course syllabus. Since 2003, the syllabus has continually evolved, setting the benchmark in the Fitness Education sector. A Personal Trainer needs a thorough understanding of both theoretical and practical aspects to practice effectively on the gym floor; this is precisely what the course aims to achieve.

State-of-the-Art Learning Environment

  • K11 Schools: K11 has dedicated learning centers, with A.V. equipped classrooms for the theory lectures, and a state-of-the-art, fully equipped gym floor as an Exercise Lab for Practical Modules.

  • K11 Exercise Labs: In addition to the K11 School’s, standalone, fully equipped Exercise Labs are available at various locations across India.  The entire theory component of the K11 Personal Trainer's course can be conveniently completed online, while the Practical Training module can be completed at any of our Exercise Labs.

Job-Ready Professionals

Through our comprehensive syllabus and teaching method, we equip our students with both theoretical and practical knowledge, as well as real-world application. This ensures that K11 Certified Personal Trainer stand out as truly job-ready professionals in the competitive Fitness Industry.
